Bitcoin for Humans

Hello, so, you’ve heard about Bitcoin. You are curious. You want to participate but you don’t feel prepared to do so. You are human. BAPP is an online Bitcoin solution that you can access through your laptop, tablet or smartphone connected to the internet. You don’t need any special gadget. You don’t have to download any app or any software. You just have to think which services you need and open a free account.

If you are just a human, this is our offer for you

We give you Security

With our non-custodial wallet, you will be the only owner of your keys, backed by a physical flashdrive, you won´t have to worry about any security breach

We take away the technical concepts

BAPP is made to make your life easier by symplifying the difficult Bitcoin concepts so you can use it as if you were using your Online Banking Account

Our User Interface

BAPP takes really serious its User Experience and Interface in order to bring the easier solution for all your problems whenever you are trading with Bitcoin

Wallet with BAPP

A total secure and easy place to you create your different personal accounts, in which you have your bitcoins and you can make your transfers and receive your deposits. Within your wallet you can open different and unlimited “accounts” for different goals, such as a Bitcoin Savings Account to easily create an account with a recurrent transfer to save Bitcoin every week or month, starting with as little as $5. Payments Account: In which you schedule recurrent service payments and one- time payments, starting with as little as $5.

Customizable View

Allows you choose between an advanced or a simple interface. You can “hide” all technical Bitcoin details or show them off.

Payment Card

Your contactless debit card gives you the power to spend your Bitcoins in relate life, anywhere Visa is accepted, and at the regular year fee Visa gives us.

Exchange both ways

Using it you can convert your regular currency to Bitcoins and backwards, paying the minimum fees possible in the world.

Escrow & Trust

If you need a third person of your choice who can temporarily hold and regulate payment in a transaction, ensuring that both involved parties adhere to the terms of their arrangement, here you go. You have templates to pick so the creation of a trust or an escrow is as easy as sending an email.

Inheritance

So, since you are the only one that knows your password (we don’t even have it), you can very easily create a will. You set the time-period you consider fair for your heirs to receive your Bitcoin assets. Set the percentage of your assets and each of your heirs. You ask them to open a BAPP Wallet with an “account” for this matter. Done.
